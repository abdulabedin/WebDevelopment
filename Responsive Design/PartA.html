Cats at the G-20 Summit

During the G-20 summit in Antalya, Turkey, in November 2015, three cats walked onto the stage of the summit. The cats smelled the flowers arranged there and refused to leave. This caused laughter among the global leaders and softened their hearts as they agreed on ensuring world peace.

Details About Cats

Cats species are small carnivorous mammals. There are about 60 cat breeds are recognized by various cat registries. Domestic cats are liked by humans because of their companionship and their ability to hunt rodents. As of 2021, there are an estimated 220 million domestic and 480 million stray cats in the world. Cats are the second-most popular pet in the United States, with 95 million cats owned. Adult domestic cats typically weigh between 4 and 5 kg. A cat can fall from heights of up to 3 meters land straight on its paws.
